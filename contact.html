Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats do you accept?

We accept most industry-standard survey data formats including raw GNSS files, LiDAR point clouds (LAS, LAZ), photogrammetry exports, CAD files, and hydrographic data. Contact us to confirm your specific format requirements.

How long does processing typically take?

Processing timelines depend on project scope, data volume, and complexity. Simple projects may take 1-2 weeks, while large infrastructure surveys might require 4-8 weeks. We'll provide a detailed schedule upon project assessment.

What accuracy can I expect?

Accuracy depends on your survey methodology, source data quality, and project requirements. We follow international standards and provide accuracy assessments for all deliverables. Discuss your specific requirements with our team.

Do you handle confidential data?

Yes. We maintain professional confidentiality protocols for all proprietary survey data. We can sign NDA agreements and ensure secure handling throughout processing and delivery.

What output formats are available?

We provide deliverables in standard formats: GeoTIFF, Shapefile, GeoJSON, LAS/LAZ, DWG, DXF, xyz, and custom formats as needed. CAD integration and custom deliverables are available.

Can you provide references or samples?

We're happy to discuss past projects and provide samples (within confidentiality agreements). Contact us to learn more about our experience in your industry.
