Precision Survey Data Processing

At Echoxis Geo Private Limited, we specialize in converting raw survey data into actionable geospatial intelligence. Our expertise spans land surveying, hydrographic mapping, and modern drone/LiDAR technologies, delivering terrain models, bathymetric maps, contours, and comprehensive survey reports to engineering consultants and infrastructure projects worldwide.

Our Core Services

Land Survey Data Processing

GNSS data processing, coordinate adjustment, contour generation, terrain modelling, and CAD surface creation for civil engineering projects.

Hydrographic Survey Data Processing

Drone and LiDAR Data Processing
