About Echoxis Geo

Precision geospatial services for engineering and environmental projects

Who We Are

Echoxis Geo Private Limited is a specialized geospatial services company dedicated to accurate survey data processing and analysis. We transform raw survey data into precise engineering deliverables that support informed decision-making across infrastructure, maritime, environmental, and engineering consulting sectors.

Our focus is on technical excellence, data accuracy, and reliable service delivery. We combine modern geospatial technologies with rigorous quality assurance to ensure our clients receive data they can trust for critical projects.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional geospatial data processing and analysis services that empower survey companies, engineering consultants, and infrastructure developers to make accurate, data-driven decisions with confidence.
